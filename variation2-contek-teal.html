FAQs

Answers to your most common questions

What types of projects does BayView Sheet Metal specialize in?
We specialize in commercial and light industrial HVAC projects, including fabrication and installation of HVAC systems, welded exhaust systems, custom sheet metal work, and flashings. We also service the construction industry throughout London and the surrounding area.
Do you fabricate custom sheet metal in-house?
Yes. All of our sheet metal work is fabricated right in our own shop. This allows us to deliver custom solutions faster and at more competitive prices than companies that outsource fabrication.
Do you service walk-in coolers and commercial refrigeration?
Yes. Our services include installation and maintenance of walk-in coolers and commercial rooftop HVAC units. We have the expertise to keep your commercial refrigeration systems running reliably and efficiently.
What areas do you serve?
BayView Sheet Metal primarily serves London, Ontario and the surrounding communities. We have been serving this region for over 30 years and are well-established throughout the area.
How do I get started with a new project?
Getting started is simple. Call us at (519) 452-3963 or email alrulton@bayviewsheetmetal.com. Our team will review your project requirements and provide a competitive estimate promptly.
