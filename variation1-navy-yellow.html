What types of projects does BayView Sheet Metal specialize in?
We specialize in commercial and light industrial HVAC projects, including fabrication and installation of HVAC systems, welded exhaust systems, custom sheet metal work, and flashings. We also service the construction industry throughout London and the surrounding area.
Do you fabricate custom sheet metal in-house?
Yes. All of our sheet metal work — including HVAC components, exhaust systems, custom fabrications, and flashings — is fabricated right in our own shop. This allows us to deliver custom solutions faster and at more competitive prices than companies that outsource fabrication.
Do you service walk-in coolers and commercial refrigeration?
Yes. Our services include installation and maintenance of walk-in coolers and commercial rooftop HVAC units. We have the expertise and equipment to keep your commercial refrigeration systems running reliably and efficiently.
What areas do you serve?
BayView Sheet Metal primarily serves London, Ontario and the surrounding communities. We have been serving this region for over 30 years and are well-established throughout the area. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.
How do I get a quote for my project?
Getting a quote is easy. You can call us at (519) 452-3963, send an email to alrulton@bayviewsheetmetal.com, or use the contact form on our website. Our team will review your project requirements and provide a competitive estimate promptly.
