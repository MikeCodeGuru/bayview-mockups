About BayView

We build systems that keep your business running.

BayView Sheet Metal, Heating & Air Conditioning Inc. is a family-owned and operated business that has served London and the surrounding area for over 30 years. We specialize in commercial and light industrial HVAC projects, delivering quality workmanship at competitive prices.

  • 01
    Family Owned & Operated
    Three generations of the Rulton family dedicated to quality HVAC and sheet metal work across London, Ontario.
  • 02
    Commercial & Industrial Focus
    We specialize in commercial and light industrial projects — from office buildings to manufacturing facilities.
  • 03
    In-House Sheet Metal Fabrication
    All sheet metal work is fabricated in our own shop, ensuring precision, speed, and competitive pricing on every job.
  • 04
    Construction Industry Partner
    We work closely with general contractors and construction firms to deliver complete HVAC and sheet metal solutions on new builds.
